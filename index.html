Tourist Places Near Us

Discover the beauty of Kodaikanal, just a short drive from our resort.

Pambar Falls

~0.5 km (Walking distance)

Also known as "Grand Cascade", these beautiful tiered falls are right in our neighborhood.

Dolphin's Nose

~0.8 km (Start of trek)

A flat rock projecting over a deep chasm, offering a bird's eye view of the valley.

Lake View Point

~1.9 km away

Capturing the entire star-shaped Kodaikanal Lake in one frame—a photographer's delight.

Bryant Park

~2.4 km away

A wonderfully maintained botanical garden featuring diverse flora and a rose garden.

Vattakanal Waterfalls

~2.4 km away

A serene waterfall surrounded by lush greenery, perfect for nature photography.

Kodaikanal Lake

~2.6 km away

A beautiful star-shaped lake perfect for boating, cycling, and horse riding.

Coaker's Walk

~3.0 km away

A paved pedestrian path providing stunning panoramic views of the distant plains.

Pine Forest

~3.8 km away

Stroll among the majestic tall pines, a favorite spot for photography and movies.

Pillar Rocks

~4.5 km away

Three giant rock pillars standing tall, often enveloped in the classic Kodai mist.

Guna Caves

~5.5 km away

Ancient caverns with unique root formations, famously known from the movie 'Guna'.
